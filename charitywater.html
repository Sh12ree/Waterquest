💧 Charity Water Mission

Deliver clean water to every villager before time runs out. Complete levels, earn badges, and help more communities!

How To Play

  • Move using Arrow Keys or WASD.
  • Walk to each villager.
  • Give everyone a water bottle.
  • Finish before the timer ends.
  • Complete levels to earn badges.
